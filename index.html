HARLEY-DAVIDSON CVO PAN AMERICA LOOKS TO BE IN THE WORKS

Harley-Davidson's upcoming CVO Pan America, revealed by the California Air Resources Board, appears to maintain the stock 1,252cc engine but is approximately 20kg heavier than the standard Pan America. Speculations suggest it might be an adventure or touring version with added accessories and luxurious detailing, typical of Custom Vehicle Operations (CVO) models.

SHOTGUN 650 PRICING ANNOUNCED, AND IT’S A BARGAIN

The Royal Enfield Shotgun 650, reviewed a month ago in LA, is now discussed for its UK pricing. The top-spec Stencil White variant is priced at £6,899, followed by the mid-spec Plasma Blue and Gree Drill at £6,799, with the base Sheetmetal Grey model costing £6,699, offering various options for prospective buyers.

SUZUKI GSX-S1000GX+ IS A TOURING-READY VERSION OF THE ‘LIFTED SUPERBIKE’

Suzuki introduces the GSX-S1000GX+, a variant of the recently launched crossover model, featuring standard panniers, similar to the GSX-S1000GT+. Priced at £15,599, it offers a £175 savings compared to buying the regular GX with panniers separately. The GX+ maintains the standard model's specs, including the K5 engine and semi-active suspension.
